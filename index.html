Getting Started

How to Play

Three moves. Endless possibilities.

1

Tap to Flap 🐦

Each tap makes your bird flap upward. Release and gravity pulls you down. Time your taps to thread through the gaps between pipes.

2

Double-Tap to Boost ⚡

Tap twice in quick succession to trigger a Speed Boost. Your bird glows gold and surges forward. Use it for those tricky tight gaps.

3

Pause When You Need To ⏸

Tap the pause button (top-right corner) anytime during gameplay to freeze the action. Tap again to resume exactly where you left off.

4

Beat Your Best Score 🏆

Every pipe you clear is worth 1 point. The difficulty ramps up continuously — how long can you survive before the gap becomes impossible?
